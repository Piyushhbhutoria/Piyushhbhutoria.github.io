<figure><img alt="Picture of a handbag." src="/blog-media/medium/handbag-4c9bb4301ddf/61a1cc4c4dc1fb.jpg" /></figure><p>It’s an amazing accessory. You never know what’s inside. It can be full of mysterious things, it could be empty. Depends on the lady who is carrying it. A student can keep her notebook, pen, Calculator, little makeup stuff, earphones, etc. Working lady can keep her necessities, umbrella, tiffin, wallet, etc. A mother with an infant in her hand can keep milk Bottle, extra diaper, wipes, handkerchief, etc. I mean it has endless possibilities. It can fit almost anything. Some keep an extra pair of clothes. You can never guess what’s exactly inside her bag. But, the things in her bag represents her, it signifies her purpose. She may keep chocolates inside for her brother, she may keep pepper spray because she travels late night, she may keep her god in it, or something to munch continuously without anyone noticing her. It shows how even a simple handbag has endless possibilities. Next time you see a lady with a handbag try to come up with possible things inside seeing her appearance. I would compare it to real-world Doremon pocket you never know what’s inside or if at all there is anything.</p>
